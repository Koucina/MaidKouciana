Splatoon

/s2 random <option> Get a random option from all weapons, subs, specials or stages

/s2 list <option> Get a list of all weapons, subs, specials or stages

/s2 schedule <mode> Get the schedule of a gamemode

/s3 map <map> <gamemode> <image> Get a stage image from many choices
