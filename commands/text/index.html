General

mk.help Global Help Command

mk.cake Gives you a cake

mk.yesorno Returns either yes or no


mk.8.ratio Ratio the Eightball

mk.8.<question> Ask the Eightball a question


mk.temp.<number><c/f/k> Convert Temperature


mk.rps.<rock/paper/scissors> Play RPS with the bot

Interaction

mk.i.hug <@user> Give someone a hug

mk.i.kiss <@user> Kiss someone

mk.2.r Splatoon 2 Turf War rotations

mk.2.a Splatoon 2 Ranked Battle rotations

mk.2.s Current Splatoon 2 Salmon Run rotation
